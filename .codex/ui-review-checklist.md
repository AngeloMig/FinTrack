# FinTrack UI Review Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing or improving any FinTrack screen.

## Visual Hierarchy

- Is the main purpose of the page obvious?
- Is the most important financial number visually strongest?
- Are secondary details quieter?
- Is there only one obvious primary action?

## Layout

- Is spacing consistent?
- Are related items grouped?
- Are cards aligned?
- Is the layout responsive?
- Is there any horizontal overflow?

## Typography

- Are headings readable?
- Are money values prominent?
- Are labels clear?
- Is muted text still readable?

## Colors

- Is contrast strong enough?
- Are colors used consistently?
- Are income and expense states clear?
- Is color supported by text/icon meaning?

## Components

Check:
- buttons
- cards
- forms
- tables
- badges
- modals
- nav/sidebar
- charts
- alerts
- empty states
- loading states

## Mobile

- Do cards stack correctly?
- Are buttons easy to tap?
- Are tables converted to mobile-friendly rows?
- Is navigation usable?
- Are forms comfortable on small screens?

## Accessibility

- Inputs have labels
- Buttons have clear text
- Icon buttons have accessible labels
- Focus states are visible
- Errors are near fields
- Touch targets are large enough
- Content does not depend on color alone
